# Candidate Communications & Scheduling Workflows

## Workflow Execution Steps

1. **Context Loading:** Load candidate details (Name, current company, key projects) and Vacancy JD.
2. **Draft Drafting:**
   - Define Subject Line (e.g. `[Candidate Name] | [Role Title] opportunity at [Company]`).
   - Compose Body with personalized hook referencing their recent experience (e.g., *"I saw your work on [Project Name]..."*).
   - Insert brief overview of our role.
   - Insert Call to Action (scheduling link or request for a quick call).
3. **Draft Safety Hold:** Save the generated email to the task log and transition task to `paused` (Needs Review).
4. **Approval Processing:**
   - On approval: dispatch message via configured email/LinkedIn integration.
   - On edit/feedback: adjust draft and re-submit for approval.
5. **Coordination:** Upon response, monitor email inbox and automatically trigger calendar invites for available time slots.

## Outreach Rules

- **Maximum length:** Keep emails under 150-200 words.
- **Tone:** Professional, enthusiastic, respectful.
- **Spam Avoidance:** Do not use sales-heavy keywords (e.g. "urgent", "huge opportunity").
